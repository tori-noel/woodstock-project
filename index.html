Why Woodstock?

Peace, love, Woodstock

Woodstock, NY is famous for the legendary Woodstock Music and Art Fair in 1969. Though the history of the festival is a huge point of pride for the town, it has myriad other things to offer! Nestled cozily in the Catskills, Woodstock bosts gorgeous hikes, vistas, swimming holes and nature walks. The town itself is bursting with culture. Lovingly curated shops and boutiques line the main street. Woodstock also has a burgeoning food and beverage scene! There are several spots for a great meal. The town is known for is relaxed, slow, comfortable environment. It's a true vacation for the busy New Yorker looking for some peace, love and happiness.
