Why Woodstock?

Peace, love, Woodstock

Woodstock, NY is famous for the legendary Woodstock Music and Art Fair in 1969. Though the history of the festival is a huge point of pride for the town, it has myriad other things to offer! Nestled cozily in the Catskills, Woodstock bosts gorgeous hikes, vistas, swimming holes and nature walks. The town itself is bursting with culture. Lovingly curated shops and boutiques line the main street. Woodstock also has a burgeoning food and beverage scene! There are several spots for a great meal. The town is known for is relaxed, slow, comfortable environment. It's a true vacation for the busy New Yorker looking for some peace, love and happiness.

Some Local Attractions

Favorite spots in Woodstock:

Garden Cafe

A friendly vegan cafe with a beautiful garden courtyard attached. Even if you're not a vegan, you're bound to love the food at Garden Cafe. They've got everything from soups and salads to a daily list of homeade desserts!

Address

6 Old Forge Rd, Woodstock, NY 12498

Why I love it

Friendly staff, delcious menu (especially the soups and desserts),truly incredibe vegan desserts and freshly brewed pots of world-class tea!

Narnia

A beautifully curated crystal and vintage shop! The staff is so warm and lovely. Find the perfect precious stone for you or a loved-one or buy a unique vintage jacket!

Address

130 Tinker St, Woodstock, NY 12498

Why I love it

The owner is so knowledgeable about crystals. Even if you aren't into using crystals in your day-to-day you are sure to be impressed by her incredible collection of precious stones, fine jewelry and unique vintage items.

Early Terrible

A true gem like no-other! Early terrible looks like the tree-house of your childhood dreams, if your tree-house served top-notch cocktails and ribeye grilled to perfection. This indoor-outdoor bar/restaurant is a favorite for so many reasons. gather a group of freinds and rent a private tent or nestle yourself by the firepit. On certain nights you can catch live musical performances, dance parties and even ariel performers!

Address

45 Mill Hill Rd, Woodstock, NY 12498

Why I love it

Absolutely delicous cocktails, atmosphere like no other and truly delicious food!
